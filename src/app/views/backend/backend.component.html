Backend Certification Training


You will be provided with a simulated Frontend Certification Exam in order to practice and improve the overall results in order to reach a positive average scores.


Rules:

  • You have 60 minutes to complete the exam;
  • You have 60 question to answer;
  • Once you answer one question you can't go back to see it again;
  • By the end of each exam you will be provided with the summary and will be able to see the correct answers for each question.
